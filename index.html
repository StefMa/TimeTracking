TimeTracking

TimeTracking is an client and server based project which can be used for time tracking.

Why?

The project was born because my Girlfriend have written all of her working time in a small notebook.
Each end of month she calculated her time spend at work and look if she reached her work time from her contract.
This was a pain!
She asked me if I want to create a small App for Android to track her working hours and calculated the time on the fly. The project was born.

TimeTracking and TimeTrackingServer is maintained with by StefMa.
